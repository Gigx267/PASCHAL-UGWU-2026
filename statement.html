artist statement

My work reflects emotions of curiosity, resilience, vulnerability and strength. After losing hearing in one ear as a child, I became more reliant on visual cues, a perspective that shapes my art today. The figures I paint, with their exaggerated eyes and single ear, represent how I experience the world. They invite deeper connection and remind us that perception is personal and varied. Through form, texture and color, I capture often-overlooked moments, celebrating the beauty of seeing and feeling life in a different way.
